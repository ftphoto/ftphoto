Hannah
Holzer

Amateur Photographer  ·  USA

I'm an amateur photographer from the USA with a love for the outdoors and an inability to leave the house without a camera. Photography is something I do purely for the joy of it.

Over the years I've been lucky enough to spend time living outside the US. I studied abroad in Ireland and Australia, and later worked for a period in Germany. Every place left me with a new set of light, landscapes, and coastlines to figure out.

I shoot mostly with a Canon EOS 5D Mark III and sometimes my smaller M50 when I want to travel lighter. Both have logged a lot of miles.

More than anything I'm drawn to water. There's a stillness that comes with standing at the edge of the ocean that I keep going back to, and that feeling tends to be what pulls me outside with a camera in the first place.
